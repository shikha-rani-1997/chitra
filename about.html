WELCOME TO CHITRAKARITA

This place is for everyone who is struggling to find their place in the world..You will find stories and mementos that I hope will touch your heart.

Painting adds meaning to your moments. It has changed me to see beyond the normal sight, to think beyond what's possible and to draw beyond reach.

Assuredly, when they write about painting, painters themselves become philosophers, and their own statements suffer from the difficulty of applying words to any one of the silent arts.

There is only one justifiable approach to painting, and it is neither archaeology, nor history, nor science, nor art criticism, nor philosophy; it is paintings

I wish to avail myself of this opportunity to express my heartfelt gratitude for you to visit my artwork.
